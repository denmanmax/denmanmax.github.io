As suggested by Myles Petersen, I decided to look into the potential to add a lower bit-rate option. I looked into what qualities Tidal currently offers, and have verified that they offer a 320 kbps option for streaming, so will include this.

As Kieran Smith gave me the idea for compressing the audio during transmission, I decided to look into if any feasible compression algorithms existed for audio or file steaming in 2006. From what I could find, there are no existing algorithms at the moment to compress audio during transmission, so any compression could only be find in the creation of the FLAC files. I also checked Tidal's current website, and they also report speed requirements that line up with my calculations, so I feel this confirms there are no compression algorithms that can ber used.
